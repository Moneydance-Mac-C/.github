## 📋 Overview

<a href="#moneydance">Moneydance</a> is a comprehensive personal finance application for Mac that handles banking, budgeting, investment tracking, and bill payment in a single application — with direct bank connections for automatic transaction download and a one-time purchase model that stands apart from the subscription personal finance market.

The <a href="#moneydance">direct bank connections</a> download transactions automatically from thousands of financial institutions — checking accounts, savings accounts, credit cards, loans, and investment accounts synchronize transaction data directly into Moneydance without manual entry. The OFX and QIF import compatibility handles institutions not supporting direct download by accepting exported transaction files from bank websites. Transaction matching identifies likely duplicates when downloaded transactions overlap with manually entered ones, maintaining clean register accuracy. The <a href="#moneydance">budget planning and tracking</a> sets spending targets by category and compares actual spending against budget in real time — monthly budget views show how spending is tracking against targets across all spending categories, with rollover budget options that carry unused budget amounts forward to subsequent months for more realistic budget behavior.

The <a href="#moneydance">investment portfolio tracking</a> monitors securities holdings, tracks cost basis, calculates gains and losses, and displays portfolio performance — handling stocks, mutual funds, ETFs, bonds, and other investment account types. Investment transactions including purchases, sales, dividends, and splits update automatically from connected brokerage accounts or through manual entry. The <a href="#moneydance">online bill payment</a> pays bills directly from Moneydance through connected bank accounts — scheduling one-time and recurring payments without leaving the application or navigating to individual biller websites.</p>

<a href="#moneydance">Transaction categorization</a> assigns categories to transactions for reporting and budget tracking — with automatic category suggestion based on payee history, rule-based automatic categorization, and split transactions for purchases covering multiple categories. <a href="#moneydance">Scheduled transactions and reminders</a> enter recurring transactions automatically — monthly bills, loan payments, and other regular transactions entered on schedule with reminder alerts before due dates.

<a href="#moneydance">Financial reports</a> generate spending summaries, income versus expense comparisons, budget variance reports, net worth trends, and investment performance reports — the reporting suite covers the analytical needs of comprehensive personal finance management. <a href="#moneydance">One-time purchase pricing</a> — Moneydance is a one-time purchase with no subscription, a meaningful differentiator in a market where Quicken and most alternatives have moved to subscription models.</p>
